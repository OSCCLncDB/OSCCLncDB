About Dr. Shelly Sehgal

Dr. Shelly Sehgal is an Associate Professor at the Centre for Molecular Biology, Central University of Jammu. Her academic interests include Human Disease Biology, Virology and Cancer Biology :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}. She holds a PhD in Microbiology and completed her postdoctoral training in Molecular Biology. Dr. Sehgal’s expertise and leadership at the CMB have strengthened research in cancer biology and molecular diagnostics at CU Jammu.
